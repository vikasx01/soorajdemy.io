1) Take 3 or 4 photos of details of an object using the Portrait mode. Try a different depth of field for each photo.

2) Capture a photo of a landscape using the horizontal panorama.

3) Take a photo of a building using the vertical panorama.

4) (If you have it available on your mobile) Shoot a Night landscape using the Night mode . If you have the option, take 2 photos trying different timers.

5) With the Manual mode (Pro mode on Android, Raw+ app on iPhone) freeze the movement of an object or person using a fast shutter speed of 1/250 or higher.
